★ Projects ★

Super Auto Pets

Date Created: 2024

Project Description: This project was created as a compliance to CMSC 22 course.
Superautopets is a game where two players separately assemble a team of pets. Each of the pets is selected for their unique abilities in preparation for battle against another team of pets. The game provides a shop that contains a collection of animals (“main collection”) that players can choose to buy from. Crucial to the game is the strategic placement of pets in the party.

Diet Solver Calculator

Date Created: 2024

Project Description: This project was created as a compliance to CMSC 150 course.
The specific objective of this project is to find the cheapest and most nutritious combination of foods that will satisfy all the daily nutritional requirements of an individual. The problem is formulated as a linear program where the objective is to minimize cost and meet constraints but will still satisfy the nutritional needs.

Farmer's Game

Date Created: 2024

Project Description:: This project was created as a compliance to CMSC 12 course.
It is a decision-making game engine where players assume the role of a farmer managing 16 different farm lots. As the farmer, you have the ability to allocate specific portions of your land to various crops. Each day, the farm faces the challenge of either plant growth or pest infestation, requiring you to make crucial decisions: whether to kill, transform, or nurture these elements. The game aims to test your strategic thinking and quick decision-making skills as you navigate the dynamic challenges of agricultural management.
